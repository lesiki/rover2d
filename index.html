Moon Rovers

Enter the initial x and y positions (integers between 0 and 10), initial direction (0 for North, 1 for East, 2 for South and 3 for West) and the instructions

Instructions can be:

  • M - Move forward 1 space
  • R - Turn right
  • L - Turn left

Sample input:

  • X: 0
  • Y: 1
  • Direction: 1
  • MMRMMLLMMM
